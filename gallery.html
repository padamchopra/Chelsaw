Playing for the keeps

With courage, We go forward

The Spirit Never Dies

Even when the winds were against us,
We kept on going. Our legacy is beginning, as
our spirit never dies and the bright light of salvation
shines in dark and empty skies

The Madness Continues

Optimism is the madness of insisting that
all is well, even when the situation is
miserable. Our stamina has never been exhausted
without a touch of madness

Keeping the flame alive

There's now a new fire in us,
If you play correctly, it warms your hearts.
and if you abuse and think we're weak,
It will burn you down to ashes

Skilled above all

Those skilled in attack move as from
above the opponent's team. Thus they
are capable of both protecting themselves
and of gaining complete victory

Champions through time

Its all those days when the body aches,
When your desire to do things is low
You feel tired, you feel restless and you feel weak
Those are the days when the champions train

Getting above inspiration

If you don't build your dreams,
Someone else will hire you to build theirs.
Thus, don't wait for the perfect moment
Take the moment and make it perfect
